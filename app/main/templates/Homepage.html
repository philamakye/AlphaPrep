Alpha Prep App



Alpha Prep App is a web-based application geared towards helping students prepare for their final examination. The app is currerntly designed for students under the West African Senior Schools Certificate Exam cirriculum.

Alpha Prep helps students take tests, see where they're lacking and suggests tutorials for them to take to better understand their course. The app also helps students track their progress with their test scores.

Alpha Prep is currently only offering tests for the core subjects, namely, English, Core Mathematics, Social Studies and Integrated Science. The webapp is user friendly and it's the hope of the designers that the users/students are able to gain help from this app to pass their examination.
